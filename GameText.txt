Zombie Survival 2077
Zombies attack the players from the four entrances Players must survive for as long as possible in this horde shooter style game. The joysticks are for basic moving along with using the top row B1 and B3 for rotating their character. Shooting is mapped to top row B2. Menu controls are Player One bottom row B1 then Player One bottom row B2 and B3 to chose the difficulty. Player 2 bottom row B1 is a restart button when you lose.
